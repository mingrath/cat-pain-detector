## Decision

`tight_content` is the least bad framing variant, but it still fails the most
important safety gate: rescue-threshold recall remains 0.0. This confirms that
cropping alone does not fix the scoring problem. The next step must be real
FGS-labeled calibration data or downgrading the project to an FGS checklist/education
demo until labels are available.
